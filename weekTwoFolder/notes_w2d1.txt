3ï¸âƒ£ CSV (tabular data)

with open("users.csv", "w", newline="") as f:
    writer = csv.writer(f)
    writer.writerow(["id", "name", "email"])
    writer.writerow([1, "Andrea", "a@ex.com"])

    reader = csv.reader(f)
    for row in reader:
        print(row)


âœ… writerow() adds one row at a time
âœ… DictWriter/DictReader let you use headers easily
